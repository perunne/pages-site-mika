About me

I'm Mika Bradley, currently a final year undergraduate student in Experimental Linguistics at University College London.

I'm mostly interested in Formal and Computational Semantics & Pragmatics, especially that of Countability, Measurement and Degrees.

I'm currently writing a thesis supervised by Yasu Sudo.

Research Interests

Broad: Formal Semantics & Pragmatics, Logic, Mathematical Psychology, Philosophy of Language & Linguistics.

Narrow: Semantics & Pragmatics of Countability and Degrees, Algebraic Semantics, Concept and Belief representation in Humans and Machines, Modal & Epistemic Logics, Functional Programming.

Service and Outreach
